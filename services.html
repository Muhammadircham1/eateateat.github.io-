Vlogging since 2018

Tanboy kun

Bara Ilham or who is familiarly known as Tanboy Kun is a YouTuber who creates content about food or is often referred to as a food vlogger. This YouTuber often makes mukbang content. Mukbang itself is taken from a term in South Korea, which means eating large portions alone. This burly man started his YouTube channel on October 27, 2016 by uploading a video containing the 2.5 kilogram Nasi Padang challenge. The video content uploaded by this man can always attract a large number of viewers. You can see that each video has been watched hundreds of thousands or even tens of millions of times. This proves that he is successful in building concepts in each of his contents. In the midst of intense competition from YouTubers, each content creator must be creative if you don't want to be left behind by the audience. According to him, being a food vlogger, especially mukbang content, is not easy because it requires sets that match the food theme. What kind of food is eaten, then the time needed during the shooting of the video, and also the ability to speak in front of the camera. In fact, must have a script during the video taking. However, this profession has brought abundant income and now he is spreading his wings by opening a culinary business..
